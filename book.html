The cardiology Department

The Cardiology Department at our hospital is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for patients with heart and vascular conditions. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology, our team of expert cardiologists specializes in diagnosing and treating a wide range of heart diseases, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects. We offer advanced procedures such as angioplasty, stenting, echocardiography, and cardiac rehabilitation programs. Focused on patient-centered care, we emphasize early diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and preventive strategies to promote heart health. With a commitment to excellence, our department ensures quality care and compassionate support for every patient.

The Orgon-transplanation

The Organ Transplantation Department at our hospital specializes in providing life-saving transplants for patients with end-stage organ failure. We offer comprehensive care for organ transplantation, including kidney, liver, heart, and lung transplants. Our highly skilled team of surgeons, physicians, and support staff ensures that each patient receives personalized care from diagnosis through post-transplant recovery. We utilize cutting-edge technology and advanced medical treatments to increase the success rates of transplants. The department is dedicated to providing hope, enhancing lives, and supporting long-term recovery with rigorous follow-up care and immunosuppressive therapy.

Our department is dedicated not only to performing successful transplants but also to offering continuous care throughout the recovery process. Post-transplant patients receive individualized follow-up care to monitor organ function, prevent rejection, and manage any complications. We focus on improving patients' quality of life and supporting them in their journey towards a healthy, fulfilling life after transplant. With a multidisciplinary approach, our team provides emotional, psychological, and physical support for both patients and their families during every step of the transplant process.
