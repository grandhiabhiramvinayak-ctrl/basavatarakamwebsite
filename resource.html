Our hospital offers advanced MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) scans to provide detailed images of the body’s internal structures without the use of radiation. MRI is an essential tool for diagnosing a wide range of conditions, including brain, spinal cord, joint, and soft tissue abnormalities. Our state-of-the-art MRI machines offer high-resolution imaging, ensuring accurate and timely results. The procedure is non-invasive, safe, and typically painless, with minimal preparation required.....

CITY SCAN

Our hospital provides advanced CT (Computed Tomography) Scan services, offering detailed cross-sectional images of the body to help diagnose a wide range of conditions. The CT scan combines X-ray technology with computer processing to produce clear images of organs, bones, and tissues. It is particularly useful for detecting injuries, infections, tumors, and internal bleeding. The procedure is quick, non-invasive, and typically painless, with minimal preparation required..

ULTRA SOUND

Our hospital offers high-quality ultrasound services, a non-invasive imaging technique that uses sound waves to create real-time images of the body’s internal structures. Ultrasound is commonly used for monitoring pregnancies, examining organs such as the liver, kidneys, and heart, as well as detecting abnormalities in soft tissues. The procedure is painless, safe, and involves no radiation. Our skilled technicians ensure your comfort throughout the process, providing clear and accurate results..

Allergy Tests

Allergy tests are used to identify substances that trigger allergic reactions in individuals. These tests can detect allergies to common allergens such as pollen, dust mites, pet dander, certain foods, or insect stings. There are different methods for testing, including skin tests, blood tests, and patch tests, depending on the type of allergy being investigated. Allergy tests help doctors create personalized treatment plans, which may include medications, lifestyle adjustments, or allergy shots (immunotherapy). Early detection through testing can improve quality of life by preventing severe allergic reactions and managing symptoms effectively..

Microbiology Tests

Microbiology testing involves the identification of microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites, that may be causing infections in the body. Common tests include cultures, where a sample (e.g., blood, urine, or swab) is grown in a lab to identify harmful pathogens. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) tests are also used to detect specific genetic material from infectious agents. These tests help diagnose infections like pneumonia, urinary tract infections, or COVID-19, enabling targeted treatment with the right antibiotics, antivirals, or antifungals...
