Our Fertility Treatments

Ovulation Induction

Ovulation Induction:
Enhance your natural cycle with targeted medication to encourage regular ovulation. This treatment helps individuals with irregular or absent ovulation by stimulating the ovaries to produce mature eggs, optimizing the chances of conception. Our team will carefully monitor your progress, adjust medication as needed, and guide you through each step for the best possible outcome. Ovulation induction can be an effective first step for those looking to improve their fertility and increase the likelihood of pregnancy. By promoting consistent ovulation, this treatment can help maximize your fertility potential and set the foundation for further assisted reproductive options, if necessary. With personalized care and ongoing support, we aim to make this process as smooth and effective as possible for you.

Comprehensive Infertility Care

Infertility Diagnosis and Treatment:
Understanding the underlying causes of infertility is crucial for developing an effective treatment plan. At Basavatarakam, we offer comprehensive infertility evaluations to help identify potential barriers to conception. Our approach includes advanced diagnostic tests such as hormone level assessments, imaging scans, and semen analysis, ensuring a thorough understanding of your reproductive health. Once a diagnosis is established, we work closely with you to create a personalized treatment strategy tailored to your specific needs. IUI Treatment

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination) Treatment:
IUI is a widely used and effective fertility treatment that involves placing prepared sperm directly into the uterus to increase the chances of fertilization. This procedure is often recommended for individuals or couples with mild fertility issues, such as low sperm count or motility, or for those with cervical mucus problems that make it difficult for sperm to reach the egg. At Basavatarakam, our experienced fertility specialists ensure that the IUI process is as comfortable and successful as possible. The treatment typically starts with hormone medication to stimulate ovulation, followed by close monitoring to determine the optimal timing for insemination. Once an appropriate time is identified, high-quality, washed sperm is carefully inserted into the uterus using a thin catheter. This increases the concentration of sperm near the egg, enhancing the chances of successful fertilization.

IVF Treatment

IVF (In Vitro Fertilization) Treatment:
IVF is one of the most advanced and effective methods of assisted reproduction. It involves combining eggs and sperm outside the body in a controlled laboratory environment to create embryos, which are then transferred into the uterus for potential pregnancy. This treatment is often recommended for individuals or couples facing infertility due to various reasons such as blocked fallopian tubes, severe male infertility, endometriosis, or unexplained fertility issues. At Basavatarakam, we provide state-of-the-art IVF treatments guided by experienced specialists who are committed to delivering the highest standards of care. The IVF process begins with ovarian stimulation, where fertility medications help the ovaries produce multiple mature eggs. These eggs are then retrieved through a minor, minimally invasive procedure.

Fertility preservation involves storing eggs, sperm, or embryos to use at a later time when you're ready to start or grow your family.
