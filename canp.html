Welcome to Our Community Health Camp!

Join us for a free health checkup and take the first step towards better health. Our team of medical professionals will be available to offer health consultations, screenings, and wellness advice to ensure you and your family stay healthy.

What We Offer at the Community Health Camp

1. Free Health Checkups

We believe in making healthcare accessible to everyone. Our free health checkups include:

  • General health screenings
  • Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Blood Sugar Testing
  • Cholesterol Level Check
  • BMI Measurement
  • Basic physical exams conducted by experienced healthcare professionals

    • 2. Discounted Lab Tests

    In addition to the free checkups, we offer discounted rates on various lab tests, allowing you to keep track of your health at affordable prices:

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Liver and Kidney Function Tests
  • Thyroid function tests
  • Diabetes screening
  • Vitamin and mineral level tests

    • 3. Free Consultation with Specialists

    Our camp features on-site consultations with qualified specialists to ensure your specific health concerns are addressed:

  • General Physicians
  • Cardiologists
  • Dermatologists
  • Gynecologists
  • Pediatricians

    • 5.Preventive Health Education

    At our camp, we prioritize education to help you stay informed and make better health decisions. We provide:

  • Workshops on maintaining a healthy lifestyle
  • Information about chronic disease management
  • Nutritional advice for balanced diets
  • Stress management and mental health tips
  • How to perform self-checks for early disease detection
