Our Services

World-Class Cardiac Care


25+Cath Labs: "Our hospital boasts over 25 specialized cath labs equipped with cutting-edge technology to provide unmatched cardiovascular care. From complex angioplasties to heart surgeries, we ensure that our patients receive world-class treatment delivered by highly skilled cardiac specialists."
Our hospital is equipped with over 25 state-of-the-art cath labs, designed to provide comprehensive cardiovascular care. These advanced facilities support a wide range of procedures, including angiograms, angioplasties, and stent placements, ensuring quick and accurate diagnoses and treatments. Each cath lab is operated by a team of highly skilled cardiologists and specialized technicians who prioritize patient safety and comfort throughout every procedure. Our commitment to cutting-edge technology and expert care has earned us a reputation for excellence in heart health, helping patients achieve better outcomes and a healthier life.

The Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)

The Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) provides specialized care for premature and ill newborns, offering services like advanced monitoring, breathing assistance, and temperature regulation. It is equipped with incubators, ventilators, and feeding tubes to support vulnerable infants. The NICU is staffed by neonatologists, specialized nurses, and respiratory therapists who deliver 24/7 care.

Family support is emphasized, with services such as kangaroo care, counseling, and education for parents. Additional services include physical, occupational, and speech therapy for developmental needs and lactation support. NICUs also offer follow-up care to monitor growth and provide referrals for long-term needs. Emergency and surgical services are available for critical cases, and clinical research is conducted to advance neonatal care.
